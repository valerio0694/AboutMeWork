Hi, I'm Valerio and I was born on 6th June 1994 in Rome, where I still live today. I have been into sports since I was a child. It all started with swimming, but I soon realized that it was not the right sport for me. Therefore I tried to play football, but my life drastically changed when I met my first love, Basketball. I devoted thirteen years of my life to Basketball and I have grown up whatching Michael Jordan. My passion for sport was so huge that it pushed me to choose the Sport Science course at University and after three years I graduated at the top of my class.

After university I started working as personal trainer at two gyms in Rome. My goals were to improve the health of my costumers and to help them achieve the body of their dreams. This is a job where you never stop studying. Every day new updates on the training method come out and to offer the best services I always keep up with them. If I had not studied Sport Science, I would have probably enrolled in computer engeneering, having always loved subjects such as math and computer science. When a friend of mine told me about the CodeMaster course I thought it was the right opportunity to learn this job.
